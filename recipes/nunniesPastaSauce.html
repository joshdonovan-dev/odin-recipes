Nunny's Sauce

My full blood italian great grandmother named Michaelna recived this recipe from her grandmother. This sauce has been passed down from generation from generation before they immigrated to America. This sauce had its origin in Naples and feeds about 10-15 people

Full Recipe

  • Large non-stick sauce/stock pot
  • Olive oil to cover base of pot
  • 2-4 garlic cloves
  • 2-3 Pork spareribs (bone-in country ribs
  • 1 pack of each sasuage (Sweet and Hot) (5 each per pack)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • 3 29-ounce cans of Red Pack Tomato Puree
  • 12-ounce can of Contadina Tomato Paste
  • 2/3 of can of water from each 29 oz can of puree
  • Oregano (dried)
  • Basil (fresh or dried)
  • Parmesan cheese
