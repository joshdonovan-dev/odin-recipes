The Perfect Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour (sifted)
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter (or margarine), softened
  • 2 ½ cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract

Frosting:

  • 6 oz semisweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup light cream
  • 1 cup butter (or margarine)
  • 2 ½ cups powdered sugar

Filling:

  • 1 cup heavy cream (cold)
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Make the Cake:

  1. In a bowl, whisk cocoa powder with boiling water until smooth. Let it cool.
  2. In another bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour three 9-inch round cake pans.
  4. In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla on high speed for 5 minutes until fluffy.
  5. On low speed, add the dry ingredients and cocoa mixture alternately. Start and end with dry ingredients.
  6. Divide batter evenly into pans and smooth the tops.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the top springs back when touched.
  8. Cool cakes in pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on wire racks.

Make the Frosting:

  1. In a saucepan, melt chocolate chips, cream, and butter over medium heat, stirring until smooth.
  2. Remove from heat and whisk in powdered sugar until blended.
  3. Place bowl over ice and beat until thick and spreadable.

Make the Filling:

  1. Whip heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until fluffy.
  2. Refrigerate until ready to use.

Assemble the Cake:

  1. Place the first cake layer upside down on a plate. Spread with half the whipped cream.
  2. Place the second layer upside down and spread with remaining whipped cream.
  3. Place the third layer right side up on top.
  4. Frost the sides first, then the top, and swirl the frosting decoratively.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. Use a sharp knife with a sawing motion to slice.
